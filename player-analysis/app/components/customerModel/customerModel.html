CustomerModel

Chisquare's customer modeling is based on the phases decribed below. This approach provides a deep understandign of the customer database and an effective means for targeting customers with personalized marketing

  

Lifecycle Stages

The top level of segmentation that Optimove performs is based on lifecycle stages.Lifecycle stages represent the different phases that customers acn go through during their relation ship with the company. The segmentation allows you to look at these segments of customers in different ways,using different metrics.To see the specific segmentation layers for each lifecycle satge below,click on the lifecycle name in the header.

  
   

Segmentation Layers

Micro Segments

 

A micro-segment is group of customers with the same "behavioral DNA", namely the same combintion of segmentation layer values.This is the most granular level of customer segmentation, and is usefull for discovering insights about small groups of customers .such as which incentives to offer them. Micro-segments also serve as the basis of Optimove's predictive modeling engine.The table below shows the characteristics of every micro-segment including its DNA ,past value,conversion rate,churn rate and reactivation rate.

Value Segments

 

value segmentation is the grouping of customers according to their expected future value,which is based on past behaviour patterns.Because value groups cut across life cycle stages,it is useful to look at a customer's value segment when deciding on policies, such as service levels and error remediation compensation levels.
